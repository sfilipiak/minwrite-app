Privacy Policy

Plain-language summary of how this app treats your data.

What data do we store?

Your writing stays on your device. This app saves text, goals, and session stats in your browser’s local storage. No account. No server-side storage.

What leaves your device?

  • Exports/Imports: When you export or import files, it happens locally in your browser.
  • Third-party assets: Fonts (Google Fonts) and libraries (unpkg for docx/mammoth) are loaded over HTTPS. These providers may receive standard request logs.

How do I delete my data?

Use your browser’s “Clear site data” or the app’s clear/reset options. This erases local storage for this site.

iOS & Mobile Notes

On iPhone/iPad (Safari), site data can be cleared automatically after about a week of inactivity. Private Browsing does not persist data. To avoid losing work, either open the app at least once a week on those devices or export your text regularly.

Cookies/Tracking

No analytics, ads, or tracking cookies.
